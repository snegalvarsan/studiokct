GROCERY MANAGEMENT SYSYTEM 

 		
Inventory is a major asset that represents tied-up capital; managing stock effectively enables a business to free up capital.
Efficient stock control requires understanding the mix of different kinds of stock and acknowledging the demands on that stock. 
This helps keep stock at a reasonable level, balancing the need for surplus supplies with the need to reduce tied-up capital.
